Fabric

We take pride in creating denim that blends timeless craftsmanship with modern innovation. Offering denim in weights from 4 oz up to 14.5 oz designed to meet a wide variety of end uses and custom requirements our fabrics are engineered to adapt effortlessly to diverse fashion applications, offering the ideal balance between strength, stretch, and sophistication. Every yard we produce is a result of precision weaving and a deep understanding of contemporary style and comfort.

At Unitywear, denim is more than a fabric it’s an expression of dedication and artistry. Our collection spans an extensive range of textures, finishes, and compositions designed to elevate every design concept. From lightweight stretch denims to structured twills, we ensure each fabric carries a unique character and uncompromising quality.

Driven by innovation and a passion for perfection, we craft denim that resonates with today’s design standards while upholding classic authenticity. Each fabric undergoes rigorous development to achieve superior feel, lasting durability, and refined appearance ensuring designers and brands alike experience quality that defines true craftsmanship.

Weave and Twill

We produce an extensive range of denim weave designs such as 1/1, 2/1, 3/1, 4/1, and 5/1 engineered for lasting performance and refined visual texture. Our weaves are available in Right-Hand Twill (RHT), Left-Hand Twill (LHT), satin, and dobby constructions, offering a distinctive surface character and reliable strength. Each fabric is woven with precision to meet modern demands in fashion and functionality.

Our denim weaving expertise covers a wide variety of patterns including 1/1 plain, 2/1, 3/1, 4/1, and 5/1 twills each created to balance durability with elegant drape. We specialize in RHT and LHT twills, complemented by satin and dobby structures, enabling unique design flexibility. Every weave is meticulously crafted to enhance depth, comfort, and resilience across diverse denim applications.

Colours And Shades

Our color palette ranges from delicate 0.5% indigo hues to deep 4.5% indigo tones, carefully curated to meet modern design demands. Offered in Pure Indigo, SBIT, and IBST dyeing technologies, along with different self-shade 30 sulfur colors, each finish is engineered to ensure precision, consistency, and richness across every denim collection.

We specialize in a versatile indigo range from the lightest sky shades to bold deep tones available in Pure Indigo, SBIT, and IBST finishes. Our advanced dyeing process maintains uniform color strength, allowing designers to create collections with exceptional depth, balance, and unique visual character across every fabric.

From soft pastel indigos at 0.5% to vivid 4.5% deep tones, our dyeing expertise covers every shade a designer could imagine. Available in Pure Indigo, SBIT, and IBST finishes along with multiple sulfur blends, each fabric is tinted with accuracy to achieve flawless consistency, superior wash performance, and enduring visual appeal.

Warp ,Weft & Blends

We offer an extensive selection of warp yarn counts ranging from coarse to superfine, available in both Ring and OE varieties. These are complemented by a versatile range of weft yarns including polyester, poly-lycra, cotton-lycra, and Tencel blends alongside sustainable recycled and organic options for modern eco-conscious collections.

Our yarn portfolio includes warp counts from coarse textures to delicate fine grades in both Ring and OE spinning. Paired with innovative weft combinations such as polyester, poly-lycra, cotton-lycra, Tencel, and sustainable recycled or organic fibers, each fabric is engineered for flexibility, strength, and superior hand feel.

From sturdy coarse yarns to premium fine counts, our warp range spans both Ring and OE varieties, designed for diverse denim structures.

The Journey of Unitywear Universal

2020 -FOUNDATION YEAR

Founded in Surat, Gujarat, as a trader company, marking the beginning of Unitywear Universal’s journey.

2022: Journey Management

Expanded operations to manage the entire process — from denim fabric selection to denim apparel manufacturing and bulk production through job work.

2025: Supply Chain

Established complete production capabilities, handling the full chain from raw denim fabric to finished denim garments, supplying global fashion houses.
